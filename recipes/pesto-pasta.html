Pesto Pasta

Description

This simple recipe only takes 15 minutes to prepare, and is bursting wih flavour. Can be enjoyed both hot and cold.

Ingredients

  • 450g of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Ground black pepper (to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with slightly slated water and bring to boil. Stir in pasta and make sure it returns to boil.
  2. Cook pasta uncoveres, stir occasionally until tender yet firm to bite (approx. 8-10 minutes). Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened (about 3 minutes).
  4. Stir in pesto, salt and pepper until warmed through.
  5. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
